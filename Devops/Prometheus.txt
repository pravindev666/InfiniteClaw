Pull Mechanism In Prometheus
After Deploying prometheus in the master machine
From master server data will be pulled
package-->node exporter will be dropped in the slave
Master will ask the node exporter in the slave for information
Master Pulls the information
Even if there is 100 server master will pull the information
the pull happens every 15 seconds this is knowns as scarape interval
the timings can be changed

TSDB---->Time Series Database
If the information is collected and dumped in the master
then the master should be big enough to accomodate the dumping

TSDB is used for Storage

Option 1-No TSDB
Shows Each and Every Value
Ex Gold Price on monday is 666
on tuesday---661
On wednesday---669

Option 2-TSDB
Shows the difference after day one
Ex Gold price on Monday-666
Tuesday=-5
Wednesday=+3 likewise
